How it works

Search that reads your documents, not the other way around

Connect your sources, ask questions in plain English, get answers grounded in your own content — with the source cited every time.

⌂

Runs on your infrastructure

Docker Compose, PostgreSQL + pgvector, local LLM inference via Ollama. No document ever transits a third-party API.

⇄

Connects where your docs already live

Google Drive, SharePoint, and Confluence connectors keep your corpus in sync on a schedule — no manual re-uploads.

✎

Answers with citations

Every answer points back to the source document and section — no hallucinated policy, no unverifiable claims.

◈

Corpus-aware access control

Separate HR, Legal, Banking, and Support corpora with their own permissions — people only see what they're allowed to.

▤

Built on Postgres

Hybrid vector + keyword retrieval on pgvector — one database to operate, back up, and reason about, not a separate vector store.

✓

Deployed your way

Self-hosted on your own infrastructure, or a dedicated instance in our cloud — same product, your choice of boundary.
